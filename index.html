Fundraising for Earthquake Victims
DISP scholars at AUPP

awareness and funds for those affected. As Myanmar scholars studying abroad, we could not assist on the ground, but we dedicated
a week to sharing verified information, reaching out to international friends and their families, and supporting donation efforts.

January 29, 2025

Termination of DISP Scholarship

International Institute of Education (iie)

The DISP Scholarship Program for Myanmar students was officially terminated on January 29, 2025. This decision follows the suspension of U.S. foreign aid programs related to diversity, equity, inclusion, and accessibility, as ordered by former President Donald Trump. The sudden cancellation has created uncertainty and concern for the future of these students, many of whom rely on this opportunity to access higher education abroad.
